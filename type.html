Helina Tea Packaging.

Packaging is what draws a customer in. It's the first impression. When first glancing at a shelf, my goal was to make this stand out from the rest. Unique shape, unique design and vibrant colors!
The top shows the hand lettered and design logo of helina tea company. The curved lettering emphasises the organic focus the company thrives on.
The organic shapes carries your around the box to view all each designed side of the box. The inside container holding the loose leaf tea is debossed to create a subtle texture.
